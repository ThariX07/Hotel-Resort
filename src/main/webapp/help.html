Staff Guidelines & System Usage

Welcome to the Ocean View Resort Reservation System. Follow the instructions below to manage guest bookings efficiently.

Navigate to the Dashboard. Fill in the guest's full name, contact number, and address. Select the desired room type and specify the check-in and check-out dates. Click Confirm Reservation. The system will automatically generate a unique RES- number and calculate the initial total cost. Please provide this RES- number to the guest.

Navigate to the Billing page. Enter the guest's unique Reservation Number (e.g., RES-A1B2C) into the search bar and click Search. The system will retrieve the guest's details, stay duration, and total amount due. Click the Print Invoice button at the bottom of the screen to send a formatted copy to the front desk printer.

Always ensure you click the red Logout button in the top right corner of the navigation bar when leaving the front desk. This terminates your secure session and prevents unauthorized access to guest data.
